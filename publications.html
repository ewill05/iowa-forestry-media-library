Project News & Announcements

January 6, 2026
Iowa Forestry Media Library Launches

The IFML archive is now live on Zenodo with the first batch of high-resolution dendrology specimens. The collection features swamp white oak (Quercus bicolor) reference scans and establishes standardized protocols for future digitization efforts.

How to Cite IFML

General Collection Citation

Iowa Forestry Media Library. (2026). Digital Dendrology Archive. Zenodo Community. https://zenodo.org/communities/ifml-archive

Individual Specimen Citation

Iowa Forestry Media Library. (2026). [Common Name] (Scientific Name) - [Specimen Type] - [Unique ID]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/[DOI]

Example: Iowa Forestry Media Library. (2026). Swamp White Oak (Quercus bicolor) - Foliage - SWO001. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.XXXXXX
